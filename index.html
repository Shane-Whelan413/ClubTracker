Features

Everything your society needs,
nothing it doesn't

Built specifically for Irish golf societies — not adapted from something else.

⛳

Stableford scoring

Hole-by-hole entry with SI-based handicap strokes. Scratch button auto-calculates minimum score. Works for 9 or 18 holes.

📊

Automatic handicaps

Set your own cuts per position — 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th. Handicaps update the moment you publish results.

🏆

Season leaderboard

League points accumulate across all events. Members see live standings, their own profile and handicap history.

📱

Add to home screen

No app store download. Members open the link on Safari, add to home screen and it behaves exactly like a native app.

💬

WhatsApp sharing

Results, invite links and playing handicap breakdowns all share directly to WhatsApp with one tap.

🎁

Extra prizes

Long drive, closest to pin, birdie prize — add custom prizes and assign winners as part of the publish flow.

🏌️

236 Irish courses

Search any Irish course by name or county and slope, rating and par auto-fill. All 32 counties covered.

🔒

PIN-based member login

Members join via invite link, admin approves and generates a PIN. No email addresses or passwords needed.
